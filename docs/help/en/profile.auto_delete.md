note_title: No warning email
note: If inactivity deletion triggers, your user is removed for good and your login stops working immediately, without a further warning.
---
An opt in setting that deletes your own user automatically after roughly six months without any activity. It affects only you, not the rest of the account.

Any activity resets the clock, and you can turn it off again at any time before it triggers.
