The account-wide view of custody: every piece you have lent out or borrowed in, in one place. A loan moves custody, not ownership, so you still own what you lend out.

Switch between "Lent out" and "Borrowed in", then use the tabs to see what is due, what needs attention, who has what, and the deposits you hold.
