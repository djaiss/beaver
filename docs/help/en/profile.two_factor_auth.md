note_title: Save your recovery codes
note: Losing your authenticator without saved recovery codes can lock you out of your user. Keep the codes somewhere safe that is not your phone.
---
Adds a second step to signing in: after your password, KolleK asks for a six digit code from an authenticator app, so a stolen password alone is not enough.

Turning it off removes the code step and deletes your recovery codes and the pairing with your app.
