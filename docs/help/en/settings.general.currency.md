id: settings.general.currency
kicker: Valuation
title: Default currency
doc: accounts.settings
note_title: Not a conversion
note: Changing the currency relabels new totals. It does not convert values you have already recorded.
---
The currency used for valuation totals across all of your collections, so pick the one you naturally think in.

You will see it wherever a total is shown, on collection statistics, dashboards, and exports.
