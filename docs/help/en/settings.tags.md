title: Tags

note_title: No undo
note: Deleting a tag removes it from every item in the account at once. Items keep their data, but the label is gone everywhere and cannot be restored.
---
Free form labels shared across the whole account, useful for slicing across items in ways categories and types do not, like "Signed" or "First edition".

Attach as many as you like to an item. A tag applies everywhere it is used, so renaming or deleting one changes every item that carries it.
