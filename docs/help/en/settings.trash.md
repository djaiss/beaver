title: Trash
doc: dataSafety.restoreFromTrash
note_title: Emptying it is permanent
note: Restoring brings an object back exactly where it was. Emptying the trash deletes everything in it for good, and nothing afterwards can be recovered.
---
Deleted collections, items, copies, categories, and sets land here first instead of disappearing immediately, kept for a retention period before they are removed for good.

Photos, documents, and copy history entries skip the trash entirely: deleting one of those is immediate and permanent.
