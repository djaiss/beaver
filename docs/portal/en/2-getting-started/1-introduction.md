# Getting Started

Welcome to KolleK. This section takes you from "I just found this" to your first catalogued item, without asking you to learn anything advanced first.

Read the pages in this order:

1. @doc(kollek.whatIs). What the product does and whether it fits you.
2. @doc(kollek.hostingOptions). How you will run it, and why there is nothing to pay inside the app.
3. @doc(accounts.create). Sign up and get your workspace.
4. @doc(auth.signIn). Every way to get back in later.
5. @doc(gettingStarted.checklist). The five things worth doing first.
6. @doc(gettingStarted.quickStart). Catalogue your first item from start to finish.

If you already know what KolleK is and just want to get moving, jump straight to @doc(accounts.create) or the @doc(gettingStarted.quickStart, "quick start").
