# Your Account and Profile

Separate from managing other people, you manage yourself: your name, your language, your preferences, and, if you are an owner, the account level settings. This section keeps those personal pages in one place.

Read the pages in this order:

1. @doc(profile.edit). Your name, nickname, email, and preferences.
2. @doc(profile.setAvatar). Upload or remove a profile picture.
3. @doc(profile.changeLanguage). Pick one of the seven interface languages.
4. @doc(activity.logAndSentEmails). Review what you did and what KolleK sent you.
5. @doc(accounts.settings). The account name, default currency, and checklist, for owners.

For security matters such as passwords, two factor authentication, and API keys, see the @doc(security.index, "Security") section instead.
