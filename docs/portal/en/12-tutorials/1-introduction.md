# Tutorials

The concept and how to pages elsewhere in this documentation teach one thing at a time. Tutorials stitch those pieces into complete, realistic journeys. Each one follows a believable collector from start to finish, explains why every step matters, and tells you what you should see when it worked.

Pick the one that matches where you are:

1. @doc(tutorials.catalogueFirstCollection, "Catalogue your first collection end to end"). Emma takes an empty account to a real Comics collection with an item, a photo, a copy, a purchase, and a valuation.
2. @doc(tutorials.setupForHobby, "Set up your account for a specific hobby"). Noah tailors types, fields, locations, and tags for his vinyl records before mass data entry.
3. @doc(tutorials.trackValuableItem, "Track the full life of a valuable item"). Priya follows one vintage watch through purchase, appraisal, insurance, paperwork, an exhibition loan, servicing, and provenance.
4. @doc(tutorials.inviteHousehold, "Invite your household or club and set permissions"). Emma brings two people into her account at different roles and shares a collection safely.
5. @doc(tutorials.selfHostWithDocker, "Self host KolleK with Docker"). Alex installs a working instance from nothing and grants the first instance administrator.

If you have never used KolleK before, start with the first tutorial, or with the shorter @doc(gettingStarted.quickStart, "five minute quick start") if you only have a moment.
