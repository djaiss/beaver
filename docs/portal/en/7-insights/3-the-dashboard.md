# The dashboard

The dashboard is the first screen you see after signing in. It is a short status report on your account: who is around, and what happened lately.

## What it shows

- **A greeting.** Good morning, afternoon, or evening, with your first name.
- **Members and invitations.** How many people are in the account, and how many @doc(collaboration.invitePeople, "invitations") are still pending.
- **Recent activity.** The latest actions across the whole account, from every member, with who did what and how long ago. This is the front window of the @doc(activity.feedAndAuditTrail, "activity feed and audit trail").

From there, your collections are always a click away in the navigation.

## The search box

The dashboard carries a search box for finding items across your whole account. Global search is not available yet; the box is a placeholder for it. Searching within one collection already works, as described in @doc(collections.chooseView). For the current status of global search, see the @doc(troubleshooting.featureStatus, "feature status page").

## Brand new accounts skip it

A fresh account with no collections is taken to the @doc(gettingStarted.checklist, "getting started checklist") instead of the dashboard. Once the first collection exists (or once an owner hides the checklist), the dashboard takes over as the landing screen.
