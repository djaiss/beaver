# Tutorials

The concept and how to pages elsewhere in this documentation teach one thing at a time. Tutorials stitch those pieces into complete, realistic journeys. Each one follows a believable collector from start to finish, explains why every step matters, and tells you what you should see when it worked.

Pick the one that matches where you are:

1. [Catalogue your first collection end to end](2-catalogue-your-first-collection.md). Emma takes an empty account to a real Comics collection with an item, a photo, a copy, a purchase, and a valuation.
2. [Set up your account for a specific hobby](3-set-up-for-your-hobby.md). Noah tailors types, fields, locations, and tags for his vinyl records before mass data entry.
3. [Track the full life of a valuable item](4-track-a-valuable-item.md). Priya follows one vintage watch through purchase, appraisal, insurance, paperwork, an exhibition loan, servicing, and provenance.
4. [Invite your household or club and set permissions](5-invite-your-household.md). Emma brings two people into her account at different roles and shares a collection safely.
5. [Self host KolleK with Docker](6-self-host-with-docker.md). Alex installs a working instance from nothing and grants the first instance administrator.

If you have never used KolleK before, start with the first tutorial, or with the shorter [five minute quick start](../2-getting-started/7-quick-start.md) if you only have a moment.
