# Getting Started

Welcome to KolleK. This section takes you from "I just found this" to your first catalogued item, without asking you to learn anything advanced first.

Read the pages in this order:

1. [What is KolleK](2-what-is-kollek.md). What the product does and whether it fits you.
2. [Cloud version versus self hosting](3-cloud-vs-self-hosting.md). How you will run it, and why there is nothing to pay inside the app.
3. [Create your account](4-create-your-account.md). Sign up and get your workspace.
4. [Signing in](5-signing-in.md). Every way to get back in later.
5. [The getting started checklist](6-getting-started-checklist.md). The five things worth doing first.
6. [A five minute quick start](7-quick-start.md). Catalogue your first item from start to finish.

If you already know what KolleK is and just want to get moving, jump straight to [Create your account](4-create-your-account.md) or the [quick start](7-quick-start.md).
