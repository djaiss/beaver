# Collaboration

A KolleK account is a shared workspace. You can keep it to yourself, or bring in your household, your club, or your business partners, each with the right level of access. This section covers bringing people in, deciding what they can do, and sharing a collection with the outside world.

Read the pages in this order:

1. [Invite people to your account](2-invite-people.md). Send an invitation and learn what the invitee experiences.
2. [Manage members and roles](3-manage-members-and-roles.md). Change what existing members can do, or remove them.
3. [The three roles in practice](4-roles-in-practice.md). A scannable reference of who can do what.
4. [Share a collection](5-share-a-collection.md). Make a collection visible to your account, or to anyone with a link.

If you have not yet read the concept page on [accounts, users, and roles](../3-core-concepts/3-accounts-users-and-roles.md), start there. It explains the model these pages build on.
