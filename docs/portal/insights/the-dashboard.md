# The dashboard

The dashboard is the first screen you see after signing in. It is a short status report on your account: who is around, and what happened lately.

## What it shows

- **A greeting.** Good morning, afternoon, or evening, with your first name.
- **Members and invitations.** How many people are in the account, and how many [invitations](../collaboration/invite-people.md) are still pending.
- **Recent activity.** The latest actions across the whole account, from every member, with who did what and how long ago. This is the front window of the [activity feed and audit trail](../core-features/activity-feed-and-audit-trail.md).

From there, your collections are always a click away in the navigation.

## The search box

The dashboard carries a search box for finding items across your whole account. Global search is not available yet; the box is a placeholder for it. Searching within one collection already works, as described in [Choose how to view a collection](../core-features/choose-how-to-view-a-collection.md). For the current status of global search, see the [feature status page](../troubleshooting/feature-status.md).

## Brand new accounts skip it

A fresh account with no collections is taken to the [getting started checklist](../getting-started/getting-started-checklist.md) instead of the dashboard. Once the first collection exists (or once an owner hides the checklist), the dashboard takes over as the landing screen.
