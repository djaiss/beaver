# Troubleshooting and FAQ

When something does not behave the way you expected, start here. These pages lead with answers and link to the detailed documentation when you want the full story.

1. [Troubleshooting sign in](troubleshoot-sign-in.md). Forgotten passwords, lost authenticators, expired links, and invitations that will not open.
2. [Troubleshooting email delivery](troubleshoot-email-delivery.md). Why an expected email never arrived, and how to check what was sent.
3. [Frequently asked questions](faq.md). Short answers to the questions everyone asks, from "what is a copy" to "how do I get my data out".
4. [Feature status and roadmap](feature-status.md). One honest page listing what is fully available today and what is still on its way.

If your question is about a specific feature, the section indexes will usually get you to the right how to page faster than these general pages.
